20% Club*

A community of engineers interested in building and shipping side projects.

We are a small community of engineers who enjoy working on side projects, with a focus on shipping. Our core values:

  • always be learning
  • done is better than perfect

The community consists of a Slack chatroom, Facebook group, and monthly meetups. Our goal as members is to hold each other accountable, share our products with each other, and build a network of enthusiastic builders. In an effort to keep the quality high with active and committed community members, you must be invited to join.

Join Now

*"20% time" is a concept popularized by tech companies that helps instill in their engineering culture the commitment towards learning, self-improvement, and experimentation, by allowing employees to spend 1/5th of their time working on whatever interests them.
